Privacy Policy

Simple, clear, and honest — because we have nothing to hide.

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

⚡ TL;DR — The Short Version
✅ We collect absolutely zero personal data
✅ Everything you enter stays on your device only
✅ No internet connection required to use the app
✅ No analytics, no tracking, no ads, no third parties
✅ Uninstalling the app deletes all your data permanently

1 Introduction

Welcome to InvoiceApp ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how InvoiceApp handles information when you use our mobile application available on Android.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our app is designed from the ground up to work completely offline — we have no servers, no cloud storage, and no mechanism to collect your data even if we wanted to.

2 Information We Collect

Information you enter (stored locally on your device only)

  • Business name, email, phone number, and address
  • Client names and contact information
  • Invoice details — line items, amounts, dates, notes
  • App settings and preferences

Information we do NOT collect

  • No personal identification data sent to our servers
  • No financial or payment information
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or crash reports
  • No cookies or tracking of any kind

3 How Your Data Is Used

All data you enter is used solely to provide the app's core functionality on your own device:

  • Creating, editing, and managing invoices
  • Generating PDF documents for you to share or print
  • Saving your business profile for reuse across invoices
  • Tracking invoice statuses locally

No data is processed by us. All processing happens entirely on your device.

4 Data Storage & Security

All data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure on-device storage (AsyncStorage). We have no access to your data — technically or otherwise.

Your data security depends on your device security. We recommend:

  • Keep your Android software up to date
  • Use a screen lock (PIN, password, or biometrics)
  • Regularly back up your device via Google Backup

5 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party under any circumstances.

The only time data leaves your device is when you explicitly choose to share a PDF invoice using your device's native share function (e.g. WhatsApp, email, Google Drive). This sharing is initiated by you and handled entirely by your operating system — we have no involvement in or visibility into it.

6 Third-Party Services

InvoiceApp does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or cloud platforms. The app makes no network requests of any kind during normal use.

7 Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data at all times:

  • Delete individual invoices at any time within the app
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data from your device
  • There is no cloud backup — deletion is immediate and irreversible

8 Children's Privacy

InvoiceApp is intended for business use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app and you have concerns, please contact us.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
